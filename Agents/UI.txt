## ASP.NET Core MVC: Before debugging CSS/JS not loading, check this first

In ASP.NET Core MVC, `~/` paths in `<link href="~/css/site.css">` and `<script src="~/js/...">` require the `UrlResolutionTagHelper` to resolve them to root-relative paths (e.g., `/css/site.css`). Without it, the browser receives `~/css/site.css` as a literal relative URL and 404s.
